# Citadex

> The world's first full-stack AEO & GEO platform — track your brand across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and 20+ AI engines.

Founded in 2025, Citadex helps brands monitor, analyze, and optimize how AI engines talk about them. Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) in one platform.

## What we do

- **AI visibility tracking** across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Grok, Copilot, DeepSeek, and 20+ AI models
- **Prompt-level rankings**, real-time alerts, and competitor share of voice
- **Multi-agent AEO content generation** + one-click publishing to 10+ platforms
- **Brand knowledge base** (RAG) — ground generated content in your real materials, not hallucinations
- **9 languages** supported end-to-end
